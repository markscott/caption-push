Fine. I’m going to give it to her and then tell her that I want to

She’s going to be the happiest little girl in the world.
WARBUCKS
Get her down here.
GRACE

Yes, sir.

Hello.

You're sending me back to the orphanage, right?

Of course not.

Annie, I was born into a very poor family and both of my

That was a good idea.

But, I’ve lately realized something. No matter how much

For me? Gee, thanks, Mr. Warbucks.
