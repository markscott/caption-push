SCENE FOUR

(Lights up. The living room of the Warbucks mansion

a couple of hours later. DRAKE, the English butler, is
supervising the SERVANTS of the house, who are bustling
about at work: CECILLE and ANNETTE, a pair of French
maids; MRS. GREER, the housekeeper; MRS. PUGH, the
cook, standing with pad and pen writing out a menu; and
four MANSERVANTS.)

(GRACE FARRELL and ANNIE enter through the door.
ANNIE is wearing a new hat and a new fur-collared coat.)

DRAKE
Good afternoon, Miss Farrell.

GRACE
Good afternoon, Drake. Everyone.

SERVANTS
Good afternoon, Miss.

GRACE
Has Mr. Warbucks arrived yet?

DRAKE
No, Miss. We’re expecting him any minute.

ANNIE
Do you really live here, or is this a train station?

GRACE
We really live here.
(to SERVANTS)
Now, would you all come here for a moment, please?

DRAKE
Quickly everyone.

GRACE
This is Annie. She’ll be with us for Christmas.
(to ANNIE)
Annie, this is everyone.
ANNIE
Hi, everyone.

DRAKE
May I take your coat, Miss?

ANNIE
Will I get it back?
