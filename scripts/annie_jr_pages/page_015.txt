All right. Breakfast.

ORPHANS

Hot mush? Yuck!

MISS HANNIGAN

No, not hot mush,

Cold mush.

And after your mush, you'll go to your sewing machines.

ANNIE, ORPHANS
Yes, Miss Hannigan.

MISS HANNIGAN
Now line up.

(The ORPHANS and ANNIE file past MISS HANNIGAN,

BUNDLES
Laundry. Laundry man.

ANNIE, ORPHANS
Mornin’, Bundles.

BUNDLES
Mornin’, kids. Clean sheets once a month, whether you need

(ANNIE, bidden by the ORPHANS, gets into the laundry
