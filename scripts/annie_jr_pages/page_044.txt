Once again we bring you the romance of Helen Trent... who
sets out to prove that just because a woman is thirty-five or

MISS HANNIGAN

God, I hope not.

GRACE
Good afternoon, Miss Hannigan.

MISS HANNIGAN

Whatsa matter, Warbucks fed up with Annie already?

On the contrary...

Miss Hannigan, this has to be signed and sent back to the
Board of Orphans no later than 10 o’clock tomorrow.

What for?

Because Mr. Warbucks is so taken with Annie that he wants to

Annie? The daughter of a millionaire?
